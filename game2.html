Candy Crush Saga

Candy Crush Saga has long been a legendary puzzle adventure game millions play today. Free and colorful, it features many levels in which you need to quickly and successfully rearrange and combine candies of different shapes in bright, colorful wrappers. The need to make deft moves against the background of rainbow cascades and enchanting colors of delicious candy combinations makes the puzzle especially attractive. Colorful pictogram causes an irresistible desire to play with such treats and be sure to eat something special. The image shows a variety of smooth and shiny candy shapes on a cheerful light blue background. The contrasting use of bright and saturated colors – the main red and a variety of small candies on the chocolate surface enhances this effect.
